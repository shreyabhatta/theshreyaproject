Why I'm doing this

Turning 25 is kind of a big deal, I guess. And I want to feel it.

There's something about turning 25. You're not quite young, not quite old, but just old enough to have something worth reflecting on.

This page is my accountability system. I'm documenting my habits, my goals, and the 25 things I've wanted to do for far too long. Why not use a milestone as an excuse to finally start?

It's personal. It's public. It's a little embarrassing. That's the whole idea.
