Why I'm doing this

Turning 25 is kind of a big deal.
I want to feel it.

There's something about round numbers that makes you want to take stock. 25 felt like a threshold — not old, not young, just enough time to have lived something worth reflecting on.

This page is my accountability system. I'm documenting my reading, my art, my habits, my goals, and the 25 things I've been putting off for too long. Not because I have everything figured out — quite the opposite. Because I think the process of noticing is itself the point.

It's personal. It's public. It's a little embarrassing. That's the whole idea.
