How to add your paintings

Upload your image files to a folder called img/paintings/ in your repo, then replace any gallery-placeholder div with a real <img> tag. The overlay title and meta below it are easy to edit in the HTML.

For the sketchbook grid, do the same — replace the placeholder cell with <img src="../img/sketches/sketch-01.jpg">.
